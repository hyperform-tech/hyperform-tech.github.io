About

What is OurEye.ai?

OurEye.ai is an Artificial Intelligence & Computer Vision powered video intelligence solution for enterprises to monitor Standard Operating Procedures and any other custom guidelines which could be monitored through the camera and ensure quality control and accelerate real-time taken from decision to action.

  • OurEye.ai is completely automated and requires minimal human intervention.
  • Leveraging pre-existing CCTV-IP Infrastructure, OurEye.ai automatically monitors your SOPs

What does OurEye.ai Provide?

OurEye.ai provides Enterprise Quality Control and automated SOP monitoring with lots of different added functionalities

  • Eaisly Adaptable: Integrateable with Existing CCTV Setup or Install New CCTV Infrastructure with OurEye.ai
  • Daily Insights: Get Insights of what is happening on your Workplace.
  • Dashboard View: Insightful Dashboard for your Entire Operations
  • Real-Time Notifications: Customisable real-time notifications for any compromise in Quality Control or Security Breach
  • One-Shot Model Training: On-Board New Employees into the system with just 2-3 images of them
